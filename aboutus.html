Our Story

Discover the heritage, passion, and craftsmanship behind El Palo Alto.

A Landmark Through Time

Established in 1409, El Palo Alto has stood as a symbol of tradition, resilience, and community for over six centuries. What began as a humble gathering place has evolved into a refined restaurant and brewery that honours its historic surroundings while embracing modern culinary excellence.

Every stone, beam, and archway tells a story — from travelers seeking rest to locals celebrating life’s milestones. Today, we continue that legacy by creating a welcoming space where guests can relax, connect, and indulge.

Our Philosophy

At El Palo Alto, we believe that exceptional dining is about more than just food — it is about experience. Our chefs craft seasonal dishes using carefully sourced ingredients, while our brewery produces distinctive beers that reflect both creativity and tradition.

Whether you are joining us for an intimate dinner, a celebration, or a casual drink, our goal is simple: to create unforgettable moments in a setting rich with history.
