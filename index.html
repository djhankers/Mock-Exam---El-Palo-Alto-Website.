Welcome to El Palo Alto

This Restaurant & Brewery is a tribute to the rich history of El Palo Alto, a landmark that has stood for centuries. The site has witnessed countless events and stories, making it a place of great significance. We invite you to explore the history and culture of this remarkable location while enjoying our delicious food and craft beers from our brewery.

Access to the site is preserved for all, providing an intimate atmosphere to enjoy the scenery.

We are open from dawn until dusk, 6 days a week. For booking inquiries or more information, please contact us at +44 7400 8175. The details can be found on our About Us or our Booking Page.

We look forward to seeing you at the landmark soon.
